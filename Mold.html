    Molding and Casting

One-Part Mold

This assignment requires creating two molds, an open-back mold and a two-part mold. Using nylon to create each mold, it requires about 6 hours for the process to dry out and create the mold. The mold for the open-cast was completed being of a vex-robotics gear andusing clear resin created a clear gear that only repliacted the properties of a gear on one side. The open-part mold was partially diffcult due to the leaking, but came out well. The two-part mold of a little ping pong ball is in process.
